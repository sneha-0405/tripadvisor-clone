If you’ve got four days to spend in Lisbon, that’s more than enough time to experience everything this capital city has to offer. And by “everything,” of course we mean taking a tram along medieval streets, riding an elevator to reach the next neighborhood, enjoying enormous plates of freshly caught seafood, and dancing until the sun comes up. There are plenty of museums and monuments, but Lisbon is mostly about doing all the magical things that locals take for granted.
